Hakone is a town on the river west of Kyoto. Many hotels called "ryokan" offer a unique tradition experience. Every hotel boasts relaxing baths, or "onsen", for their guests to enjoy as well as top tier food & hospitality.

art & beauty

The hakone open air museum is one of the most spectacular and special art museums in the world. The experience of being in a giant sculpture garden and having the ability to interact with the work of world-renowned artists is truly one-of-a-kind.

Hakone Mountain Train, Hakone, Japan

Black Eggs, Hakone, Japan

From the open air museum you can take a train to a funicular to a gondola and up a hill over the steaming sulfur pits. You can get out at the top and eat an egg that has been cooked with the sulfur steam. These eggs are black and allegedly add 7 years to your life per egg consumed!
