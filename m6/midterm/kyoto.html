Nara Deer, Nara

Oh, Deer!

Nara is a short train ride away from tokyo. The deer in Nara roam free by the hundreds. The locals will sell you cookies to feed them. Beware, you may lose a finger - these deer are hungry. There are also hundreds of school children visiting the temple in town that houses Japan's largest Buddha, best not to feed them either.
